Art Lessons

Private Painting Lessons

Do you need some help getting started with your painting?

Do you feel too self conscious to join up with a large class?

If you have a paint related event in mind, please do call! I can provide one on one lessons for any age group, or small party. Suitable for hen nights, baby showers, or birthday parties. Adaptable for all ages between 5 and 100, though children under 14 should be accompanied by an adult.

How about an evening recreating The Starry Night while listening to Vincent van Gogh's life history and how he came to be who he was?

Please email me for Private Painting Lesson availability and pricing.

Still Life Studio Time

On Wednesday every week, I hold a Still Life painting session for the more experienced painter who might lack the space to practice ... all materials supplied. Adults only, please.

Please email me for Still Life Studio availability and pricing.

Sip and Paint Group painting

Come join us on Fridays for an informal evening of chatting and guided painting, a glass of wine, and nibbles with friends.

Sip and Paint sessions are perfect for girls' night out, or a relaxed opportunity to meet with other new painters. Suitable for fun lovers over 18.

Please email me for Sip and Paint availability and pricing.
